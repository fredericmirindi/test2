Frederic Mirindi is a PhD Candidate in Economics & Econometrics at the University of Manitoba. He is a leading researcher specializing in artificial intelligence applications in economic analysis. With extensive experience in machine learning models for economic forecasting and policy analysis, he has pioneered innovative approaches to understanding economic phenomena through computational methods.
